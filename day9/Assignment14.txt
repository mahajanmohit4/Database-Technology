1) Write a command that puts the following values, in their given order, into the
salespeople table: city – San Jose, name – Blanco, comm – NULL, cnum – 1100.
query:-	insert into salespeople (city,sname,comm,snum)
	values('San Jose','Blanco',NULL,1100);

2) Write a command that removes all orders from customer Clemens from the
Orders table.
query:-	delete from orders
	where cnum = any (select cnum from customers
			where cname = 'clemens');

3) Write a command that increases the rating of all customers in Rome by 100.
query:-	update customers
	set rating=rating+100;

4) Salesperson Serres has left the company. Assign her customers to Motika.
query:-	update customers
	set snum= (select snum from salespeople
   			where sname='serres')
	WHERE snum = (select snum from salespeople
     			where sname='Motika');
